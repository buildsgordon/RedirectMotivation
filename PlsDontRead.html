Stop scrolling. Stop wasting your life.

What the hell are you doing? You already know it’s stupid, yet you keep throwing away the only resource you’ll never get back—time.

Want to matter? Then act like it.

  • Work now.
  • Finish fast.
  • Push harder than you think you can.
  • Build yourself into someone worth respecting.

You don’t deserve greatness if you can’t even fight your own laziness.
